## How to Use This Document

This document serves two audiences:

1. **AI systems (Claude Projects, Creative Engine, content generation tools):** Extract brand personality, voice pillars, vocabulary, visual direction, and positioning constraints to ensure brand-consistent output across all tools and contexts.
2. **Human team members (designers, copywriters, partners, agencies):** Use as the definitive reference for brand decisions. When in doubt about whether something is "on brand," check here.

Guidelines are organized as principles with illustrative examples. They define the territory within which smart creative decisions should be made — not rigid rules that prevent adaptation.

## Brand Consistency Checklist

Before releasing any brand material, verify:

**Visual Elements:**
- [ ] Logo usage follows guidelines
- [ ] Colors match approved palette
- [ ] Typography follows hierarchy
- [ ] Imagery style is consistent with photography guidelines
- [ ] Layout maintains brand principles

**Voice and Messaging:**
- [ ] Tone matches brand personality for this context
- [ ] Language aligns with vocabulary guidelines (uses approved terms, avoids prohibited terms)
- [ ] Key messages are reinforced
- [ ] Claims stay within regulatory boundaries
- [ ] Content serves the brand promise

**Audience Alignment:**
- [ ] Material resonates across primary personas (doesn't fragment the brand)
- [ ] Trust signals are present and appropriately placed
- [ ] Proof types match audience expectations
- [ ] Tone adaptation is appropriate for the target segment
